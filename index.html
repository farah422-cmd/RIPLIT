Welcome

Meet Filsan Ahmed

Welcome to Aesthetic by Filsan — a premier beauty studio launching in the Twin Cities this Fall 2025. I'm Filsan Ahmed, a licensed esthetician with over a decade of experience in skincare and wellness, specializing in advanced facial technology, chemical treatments, and multicultural skincare.

My mission is simple: to provide cutting-edge, non-invasive esthetic treatments in a tranquil, professional setting — and to make luxury skincare accessible and inclusive for everyone. With multilingual staff and a deep commitment to culturally competent care, our studio is a space where every client feels seen, valued, and beautifully served.

Whether you're addressing acne, hyperpigmentation, aging, or simply want to glow — I'll build a personalized treatment plan around your unique skin and your unique story. Come as you are. Leave radiant.

— Filsan Ahmed, LE
