About Us

The First Center of Its Kind

The Center for AI Inclusion was founded on a powerful premise: the communities historically left out of technological revolutions must be at the center of the AI one.

We are the nation's first organization exclusively focused on creating AI literacy, career pathways, and equitable access for Somali youth, East African immigrants, and other underrepresented communities. We don't just talk about inclusion — we build it.

  • AI Literacy & Education Hands-on AI training requiring zero prior technical background
  • Career Pathways Direct pipelines to internships and jobs in the AI & tech industry
  • Community Empowerment Building local AI ecosystems within underserved neighborhoods

AI Youth Bootcamp

An intensive 8-week program introducing Somali youth ages 14–22 to machine learning, data science, and AI tools. No prior coding experience needed.

Who We Serve

Built For Our Community

Our programs are designed from the ground up to meet the specific needs, cultural contexts, and aspirations of the communities we serve.

01

Somali Youth

Ages 14–24, especially first and second-generation Somali Americans ready to break into technology and AI careers.

02

East African Communities

Ethiopian, Eritrean, Kenyan, and other East African community members seeking AI literacy and meaningful technology skills.

03

First-Generation Students

Students and recent graduates who are the first in their families to enter the professional tech workforce.

04

Underrepresented Professionals

Working adults from underrepresented backgrounds looking to transition into or advance within the rapidly growing AI field.
