Welcome

Meet the Founder

Welcome to Aesthetic Studio by Filsan — a sanctuary dedicated to helping you look and feel your absolute best. I'm Filsan, a licensed esthetician and skincare specialist with over 8 years of experience transforming skin and restoring confidence.

My approach is deeply personal. Every client who walks through our doors receives a thorough skin consultation and a treatment plan crafted specifically for their unique needs. I believe that beautiful skin is not a luxury — it's a result of the right care.

Located in the heart of Golden Valley, MN, our studio is designed to be your peaceful retreat from the everyday. Come as you are — leave radiant.
