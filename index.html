Our Mission

Built to Close the Gap

AI is no longer the future — it’s the present. Yet the communities who stand to gain the most from AI are often the least equipped to access it. The Center for AI Inclusion exists to change that.

We build bridges between underserved communities and the AI-driven economy through education, workforce development, entrepreneurship, and advocacy.

“

I believe where every person, regardless of income, race, language, or background, has the knowledge, skills, and opportunities to thrive in an AI-driven world.

— Center for AI Inclusion
Who We Are

Inclusion

Every community deserves equal access to AI education and opportunity, regardless of background.

Innovation

We develop new models for community-centered AI education that meet people where they are.

Equity

We actively work to dismantle barriers that prevent marginalized communities from thriving in tech.

Leadership

We cultivate AI leaders from within communities, ensuring diverse voices shape the future of technology.

Collaboration

We partner with employers, educators, governments, and community organizations to create lasting change.

Strategic Framework

Five Pillars of Opportunity

Our work is organized around five interconnected pillars that together create equitable access to the AI-driven economy for every community member.

01

AI Access

Removing technological and economic barriers so every community member can access AI tools, resources, and opportunities.

02

AI Literacy

Building foundational AI knowledge through community workshops, bootcamps, and multilingual education programs.

03

Workforce

Creating direct pathways from our programs to AI-sector employment through employer partnerships, internships, and career coaching.

04

Entrepreneurship

Supporting community members to launch AI-enabled businesses and social ventures that serve their own neighborhoods.

05

Leadership

Elevating diverse voices into AI policy, industry, and academia so tomorrow’s AI ecosystem reflects all of humanity.

Core Programs

Three Pathways to the Future

Each program is purpose-built for a specific community need — from AI literacy and workforce entry to entrepreneurship and civic leadership.

Workforce Pathway

AI Opportunity Corps

An intensive training program that equips participants with practical AI and technology skills for immediate workforce entry. Focused on youth development, AI literacy, and sustainable employment.

  • AI & Tech Skills Training
  • Youth Development Focus
  • Job Placement Support
  • Continuous Evaluation
Learn more
Community Program

AI New Americans

Designed specifically for immigrants and refugees, this program delivers AI literacy and digital skills training in multiple languages — including Somali, Arabic, and English — meeting communities exactly where they are.

  • Multilingual (Somali, Arabic, English)
  • Immigrant & Refugee Focused
  • Digital Safety & Privacy
  • Family-Centered Learning
Learn more
Entrepreneurship Pathway

AI Entrepreneurship Lab

A dedicated space for community members to build AI-enabled businesses that solve real challenges in their neighborhoods, with support for customer service, market research, and business growth.

  • AI-Powered Business Tools
  • Customer Service & Marketing AI
  • Market Research Support
  • Business Growth Mentorship
